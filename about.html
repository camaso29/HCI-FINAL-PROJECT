The GC Exchange Hub

introduces a specialized platform intended

for Gordon College students to resell their unused items

such as uniforms, books, and more.


Our objective is to offer

a convenient and effective resolution to the

common struggle of locating appropriate channels

for selling items within the campus community.

By doing so, we aim to promote sustainability

and enhancecommunity engagement

among students.
